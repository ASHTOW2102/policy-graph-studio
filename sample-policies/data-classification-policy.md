# Data Classification and Handling Policy

## Data Classes
Company information must be classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted.

## Confidential Data
Confidential information includes internal policy documents, pricing strategy, security procedures, employee records, and non-public operational reports.

## Storage Requirements
Confidential and Restricted documents must be stored only in approved company repositories with access controls enabled.

## Local Storage
Users must not download Confidential or Restricted files to unmanaged personal storage, personal cloud drives, or removable media unless an exception has been approved by Information Security.

## Sharing Rules
Confidential documents may be shared only with personnel who have a valid business need and approved access rights.

## External Parties
Third parties and contractors must use approved company channels to review Confidential documents. Sending Confidential documents to personal email accounts is prohibited.
