# Security Incident Response Policy

## Reporting Obligation
Employees, contractors, and vendors must report suspected security incidents immediately to the Security Operations team.

## Lost Devices
Any lost or stolen device that had company access must be reported within one hour of discovery.

## Containment
Security may disable accounts, revoke sessions, block endpoints, or restrict network connectivity to contain risk.

## Evidence Preservation
Users must not delete logs, emails, screenshots, or related files that may be required for investigation.

## Communication
Only authorized spokespersons may communicate externally about a confirmed security incident.
