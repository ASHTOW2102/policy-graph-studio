# Employee and Contractor Access Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how employees, contractors, and third-party support staff may access internal systems and confidential company information.

## Approved Access Methods
Access to internal systems is allowed only through approved company-managed devices, approved virtual desktop environments, or explicitly approved secure remote access services.

## Contractor Restrictions
Contractors may access only the systems and documents required for their assigned work. Contractor access must be approved in writing by the hiring manager and the system owner before access is granted.

## Personal Device Rule
Personal laptops, tablets, and mobile devices must not be used to access confidential policy documents, internal administration portals, or regulated customer data unless an explicit exception is approved by the Information Security team and recorded in writing.

## Authentication
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all remote access sessions, including contractor access.

## Session Controls
Users must not share credentials, browser sessions, or one-time passcodes. Idle remote sessions must time out after the approved inactivity period.

## Exceptions
Any exception must include the business reason, approved duration, systems in scope, and compensating controls.
